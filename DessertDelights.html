Dessert Delights

Satisfy your sweet tooth with our Dessert Delights — a heavenly collection of traditional Thai sweets and refreshing treats. The perfect way to cool down after a spicy meal or to enjoy as a delightful afternoon pick-me-up.

  • Mango Sticky Rice (Khao Niew Mamuang) — Sweet Jasmime sticky rice infused with rich coconut milk, served with ripe, juicy Nam Dok Mai mango.
  • Coconut Ice Cream — House-made creamy coconut milk ice cream, topped with roasted peanuts and sweet corn.
  • Tubtim Krob (Red Rubies) — Crispy water chestnuts in tapioca flour, served with crushed ice and aromatic sweet coconut syrup.
  • Thai Tea Crepe Cake — Delicate layers of thin crepes layered with smooth pastry cream, drizzled with a rich Thai tea sauce.
  • Banana in Coconut Milk (Kluay Buat Chi) — Tender bananas simmered gently in a warm, sweet, and slightly salty coconut cream.

*Our desserts are made fresh daily with low-sugar options available upon request. Please ask our staff for today's special sweet treats!
