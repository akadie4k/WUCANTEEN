Drinks & Beverages

Refresh yourself with our Drinks & Beverages selection. From our famous freshly brewed Thai milk tea to traditional herbal infusions and ice-cold refreshments, we have the perfect drink to complement your dining experience.

  • Thai Iced Milk Tea (Cha Yen) — Strongly-brewed premium Thai tea, sweetened and layered with creamy evaporated milk.
  • Thai Iced Lemon Tea (Cha Manao) — Refreshing Thai black tea infused with fresh lime juice and a touch of sweetness.
  • Fresh Coconut Water — Sweet, chilled juice served straight from a young green coconut.
  • Lemongrass Pandan Infusion — A soothing, fragrant herbal drink brewed with fresh lemongrass stalks and pandan leaves.
  • Onyx Roselle Juice (Nam Kra-Jieb) — Sweet and tart herbal tea made from dried roselle calyces, served over ice.

*Sweetness levels can be customized (e.g., Unsweetened, Less Sweet, or Normal). Just let our barista know your preference!
