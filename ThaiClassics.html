Thai Classics

Welcome to Thai Classics — a curated selection of Thailand's most beloved dishes, prepared with authentic spices, fresh herbs, and traditional techniques. Whether you prefer mild and aromatic or bold and spicy, there's something here to satisfy every palate.

  • Pad Thai — Stir-fried rice noodles with tamarind, tofu, egg, and peanuts.
  • Green Curry (Gaeng Keow Wan) — Creamy green curry with coconut milk and basil.
  • Tom Yum Soup — Spicy and sour soup with lemongrass, lime, and shrimp.
  • Massaman Curry — Mild, slightly sweet curry with potatoes and peanuts.
  • Pad Kra Pao — Stir-fried holy basil with your choice of protein and chili.

All dishes can be customized for spice level and dietary preferences. Ask our staff for recommendations.
