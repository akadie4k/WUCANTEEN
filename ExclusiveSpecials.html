Exclusive Specials

Welcome to Exclusive Specials — our chef's signature creations that elevate traditional Thai flavors into a premium culinary experience. Crafted with seasonal, luxury ingredients and modern presentation, these limited-time dishes are designed to amaze your senses.

  • Lobster Pad Thai — Our classic tamarind rice noodles topped with a whole grilled Phuket lobster.
  • Wagyu Beef Massaman — Ultra-tender A5 Wagyu beef slow-cooked in a rich, aromatic spiced curry with purple sweet potatoes.
  • Truffle Holy Basil (Khao Pad Kra Pao Truffle) — Stir-fried minced Kurobuta pork with wild holy basil, enhanced with premium black truffle paste.
  • Crab Meat Fried Rice — Premium Jasmine rice wok-fried with generous chunks of fresh jumbo lump crab meat.
  • Khao Soi with Roasted Duck — Northern Thai golden curry noodle soup topped with crispy-skinned succulent roasted duck breast.

*These exclusive dishes are subject to availability. Please ask our staff about today's special selection and spice adjustments.
